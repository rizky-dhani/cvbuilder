# Initial Concept

To be a comprehensive career management platform with CV versioning and job tracking.

# Product Guide - CV Builder

## Vision
The CV Builder is a comprehensive career management platform designed to go beyond static resume generation. It empowers users to manage their entire career trajectory through robust CV versioning, application tracking, and achievement logging.

## Core Goals
- **Master Record Management:** Provide a central repository for all professional experiences, skills, and accomplishments.
- **Dynamic Versioning:** Allow users to create, store, and iterate on multiple versions of their CV tailored for specific industries or roles.
- **Application Lifecycle Tracking:** Enable users to track job applications, from submission to interview and offer, linked to the specific CV version used.
- **Professional Presentation:** Ensure all generated CVs adhere to high standards of professional design and are exportable in industry-standard formats.

## Key Features
- **Centralized Profile:** A unified dashboard to manage work history, education, skills, and certifications.
- **CV Builder Engine:** An interactive interface to compose resumes by selecting and arranging components from the master profile.
- **Version Control:** Save "snapshots" of CVs and track which version was sent to which employer.
- **Job Application Tracker:** A Kanban-style or list-based tracker to manage the status of job applications.
- **Analytics/Insights:** (Optional) Basic metrics on application success rates and skill gaps.

## Target Audience
- **Active Job Seekers:** Individuals managing high volumes of applications who need to keep their documents organized and targeted.
- **Career-Oriented Professionals:** Users looking to document their achievements in real-time to prepare for future opportunities.
- **Freelancers/Consultants:** Professionals who frequently tailor their profiles for different client engagements.
